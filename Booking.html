Equipment Reservation

Secure your time on our advanced metrology systems. Please review all safety protocols before booking.

Before You Book

  • Safety Training: You must have completed the EHS Basic Lab Safety module within the last 12 months.
  • Gowning Protocol: Proper cleanroom attire (bunny suit, hair net) is required for Bay 1 & 2 access.
  • Certification: Only authorized users may book the JEOL TEM and Keyence VHX. Contact staff for training.

Lab Policies

Cancellation: Must cancel at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a "No-Show" fee.

Late Arrival: Reservations are forfeited after 15 minutes. Equipment will be released to walk-in users.

Data Storage: Users are responsible for backing up their data. Local drives are wiped weekly.
